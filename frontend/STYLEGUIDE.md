# MediBook Frontend Style Guide

## Brand Vision
MediBook is a premium, modern healthcare SaaS platform. The design should feel clean, trustworthy, and efficient.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (sans-serif)
- **Scale**:
  - `h1`: 2.25rem (36px) / 2.5rem lead
  - `h2`: 1.875rem (30px) / 2.25rem lead
  - `h3`: 1.5rem (24px) / 1.75rem lead
  - `base`: 1rem (16px) / 1.5rem lead
  - `sm`: 0.875rem (14px) / 1.25rem lead

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#2563eb` (Blue-600) - Primary actions, brand elements
- **Primary Hover**: `#1d4ed8` (Blue-700)
- **Success**: `#16a34a` (Green-600) - Completed states, confirmations
- **Warning**: `#f59e0b` (Amber-500) - Pending states, cautions
- **Danger**: `#dc2626` (Red-600) - Cancellations, errors
- **Background**: `#f8fafc` (Slate-50)
- **Surface**: `#ffffff` (White)
- **Text Primary**: `#0f172a` (Slate-900)
- **Text Secondary**: `#475569` (Slate-600)
- **Border**: `#e2e8f0` (Slate-200)

## Components
### Buttons
- **Primary**: Solid Blue-600, White text, 8px radius
- **Secondary**: Slate-100 bg, Slate-900 text
- **Outline**: Slate-200 border, Slate-900 text

### Skeletons
Use a gentle pulse animation with `#f1f5f9` background.

### Status Badges
- **Pill shape**, 12px font size, semi-bold.
- `PENDING`: Bg Amber-100, Text Amber-700
- `CONFIRMED`: Bg Blue-100, Text Blue-700
- `COMPLETED`: Bg Green-100, Text Green-700
- `CANCELLED`: Bg Red-100, Text Red-700

## Layout
- **Sidebar Width**: 280px
- **Header Height**: 64px
- **Max Content Width**: 1280px
- **Border Radius**: 12px (standard cards), 8px (inputs/buttons)
